Welcome to our coffee stand!

Breaking the Silence is a committee within SCOME of IFMSA-VU that focuses on raising awareness and understanding for the deaf and hard of hearing. Today, we have a coffee stand offering free coffee, tea, cookies, and fruit. The only condition is that you place your order in sign language! Don't worry, we'll teach you how.

This way, we want to introduce people to Dutch Sign Language (NGT) and support the deaf and hard of hearing community.
